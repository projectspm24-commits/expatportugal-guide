Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

ExpatPortugal.guide is a community-driven website for English-speaking expats in Portugal. We are operated as a personal project and are not a registered company at this time.

What data we collect

We collect only the data you voluntarily provide:

Email addresses — when you subscribe to our newsletter, post a housing listing, submit a place, or send a message through the platform.

Listing information — business names, descriptions, cities, and contact details you provide when listing a place, service, or housing.

Messages — when you contact a housing lister through our platform, we store the message to facilitate the connection.

How we use your data

Email addresses are used to send our Sunday Digest newsletter (via Buttondown) and, for housing listers, to facilitate inquiries about their listings. We do not sell, share, or rent your email address to third parties.

Payments

Payments for housing listings and business claims are processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card information. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment processing.

Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies or analytics cookies. We use Vercel for hosting, which may set functional cookies. No advertising cookies are used.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services: Supabase (database), Vercel (hosting), Stripe (payments), Buttondown (newsletter), Anthropic (AI chat). Each has their own privacy policy.

Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, email us at hello@expatportugal.guide.

Newsletter unsubscribe

Every newsletter email contains an unsubscribe link. You can also email us to be removed.

Contact

For privacy questions: hello@expatportugal.guide
