⚠️ Important: This board is for medium-term rentals (30+ days) only. Short-term tourist rentals require an Alojamento Local (AL) licence. If you are subletting, Portuguese law requires written permission from your landlord, and you may not charge more than 120% of your own rent. ExpatPortugal.guide is a classifieds board only — we do not verify listings, handle payments, or act as party to any rental agreement. Always sign a written contract and verify the landlord's right to rent the property.
