💡 Tips for joining expat communities
• Show up to your first event alone — everyone else did too, and they'll remember what that feels like.
• Try a Portuguese-speaking event too, even if your Portuguese is basic. Locals appreciate the effort.
• Don't just join online groups — the real connections happen in person.
• Give it 3 meetups before deciding if a group is for you. The first one is always awkward.
• Start your own! If there's no surf group in Peniche, create one. Others are looking too.
