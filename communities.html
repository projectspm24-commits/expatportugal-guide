💡 Tips for joining expat communities
• Don't worry about showing up to your first event alone — many others did too. It's the best way to meet new people.
• Consider trying a Portuguese-speaking event, even if your Portuguese is basic. Locals really appreciate the effort, and you'll pick up more than you think.
• Online groups are great for info, but the real friendships tend to happen face-to-face. Try to make it to an in-person meetup.
• Give a group at least 2–3 meetups before deciding if it's for you. The first one always feels a bit different.
• Can't find what you're looking for? Start your own group! Chances are others nearby are looking for the same thing.
